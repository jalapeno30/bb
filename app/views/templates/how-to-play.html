Standard Play

Select the number of draws.

Pick six numbers from 1 to 45 and mark with a vertical line.

Mark LP for Lucky Pick.

If an error is made, mark the VOID box to indicate that the board is cancelled.

For only twenty pesos (Php20.00), you win if you match 6 or any 3, 4, or 5 numbers of the winning 6-number draw combination.

If there are more than one jackpot winner, the jackpot prize will be divided equally among the winners. In the event that there are no winners for the jackpot, said prize is added to the pot of the next scheduled draw.

Jackpot Prize = Minimum Jackpot Prize of Php 9 Million
Second Prize = Up to Php 50,000.00
Third Prize = Up to Php 1,500.00
Fourth Prize = Php 20.00

Advance Play

Avoid the long wait to be able to buy your tickets by playing the advance draws which allows you to play from 2 up to 6 consecutive draws. Simply mark the number of Advance Draws you want to play under DRAW panel, your ticket will automatically be valid for the current draw and the succeeding draws, depending on the number of draws you marked.

For example:
3 Draws = ticket is valid for current draw and two (2) consecutive advance draws.

Sytem Play

You may increase your chances of winning by the System Play. Simply mark the system you want on your System Play panel and select the corresponding numbers in your game panel.

5-Roll (5R)

For only Php800.00, you may select five (5) numbers from 1 to 45 on your game panel. The computer selects the 6th number from the remaining 40 numbers you did not choose from, giving you a total of forty (40) six-number combination in the ordinary game.

System 7

It you have seven (7) numbers and you don’t know which number to drop, play System 7. The computer will give you all the possible six-number combinations from your seven (7) numbers. With System 7, seven (7) six-number combinations will be generated.

For example:
Your seven (7) numbers are 02 – 05 – 17 – 23 – 29 – 36 – 41. All the possible six-number combinations are:

05 – 17 – 23 – 29 – 36 – 41

Lucky Pick

If you want to try any of the System Play(s) but you don’t have any number in mind, you can let the computer choose your numbers by playing Lucky Pick (LP).

Just mark your choice of System Play and Lucky Pick (LP) on your play slip

For example:

5Roll, Lucky Pick – Mark 5R and LP on your play slip, pay Php800.00 for forty (40) combinations.

System 7, Lucky Pick – Mark SYS 7 and LP on your play slip, pay Php140.00 for seven (7) combinations.

The same principle applies to System 8, 9, 10, 11 and 12, Lucky Pick.
