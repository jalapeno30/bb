HOW TO PLAY GRANDLOTTO 6/55

The Power to Play

Get a GrandLotto 6/55 playslip and mark 6 numbers from numbers ranging from 1-to-55 or simply give your 6 number combination to your lotto operator or you can use Lucky Pick (LP). For standard 6 number play, minimum play is Php20.00.

You can also play the different System plays:

Game Type Cost (Peso)
System 5R or 5 roll 1,000.00
System 7 (7 combinations) 140.00
System 8 (28 combinations) 560.00
System 9 (84 combinations) 1,680.00
System 10 (210 combinations) 4,200.00
System 11 (462 combinations) 9,240.00
System 12 (924 combinations) 18,480.00

Advanced play up to six (6) consecutive draws is also available.

Schedule of draw days are Monday, Wednesday and Saturday.
Available only to players 18 years of age and above.

Jackpot Prize = Minimum Jackpot Prize of Php 30 Million
Second Prize = Up to Php 200,000.00
Third Prize = Up to Php 3,000.00
Fourth Prize = Php 20.00

For only Php20.00, you may win if you match 6 or any 3, 4, or 5 numbers of the winning 6-number draw combination.

Prizes amounting to Php5,000.00 and below can be claimed at any authorized Lotto outlet or at the PCSO Provincial District Offices in your area.

Prizes worth more than Php5,000.00 to Php20,000.00 can be claimed at any of the PCSO Branch Offices or at the PCSO Head Office, PICC Secretariat Bldg, CCP Complex, 1307 Roxas Blvd., Pasay City.

Jackpot prize should validated and claimed at the PCSO Head Office, PICC Secretariat Bldg, CCP Complex, 1307 Roxas Blvd., Pasay City.

HOW TO PLAY SUPERLOTTO 6/49

Standard Play

Select the number of draws you want to play.

Pick six numbers from 1 to 49 and mark with a vertical line. Mark LP for Lucky Pick.

If an error is made, mark the VOID box to indicate that the board is cancelled.

For only twenty pesos (Php20.00), you win if you match 6 or any 3, 4, or 5 numbers of the winning 6-number draw combination.

If there are more than one jackpot winner, the jackpot prize will be divided equally among the winners. In the event that there is no winner for the jackpot, said prize is added to the pot of the next scheduled draw.

Jackpot Prize = Minimum Jackpot Prize of Php 16 Million
Second Prize = Up to Php 70,000.00
Third Prize = Up to Php 2,000.00
Fourth Prize = Php 20.00

The Superlotto Draws can be seen every Tuesday, Thursday and Sunday at 9:00 p.m. over PTV 4 together with your other favorite Lotto games.

Advance Play

Avoid the long wait to be able to buy your tickets by playing the advance draws which allows you to play from 2 up to 6 consecutive draws.

Simply mark the number of draws you want to play under DRAW and your ticket is valid for the current draw and the succeeding draws depending on how many draws you marked.

For example:
3 Draws = ticket is valid for current draw and two (2) consecutive advance draws.

Sytem Play
5-Roll

For only Php880.00, you may select five (5) numbers from 1 to 49 on your game panel. The computer selects the 6th number from the remaining 44 numbers you did not choose from, giving you a total of 44 six-number combination in the ordinary game.

System 7

If you have seven (7) numbers and you don’t know which number to drop in order to play System 7. The computer will give you all the possible six-number combinations from your seven (7) numbers. With System 7, seven (7) six-number combinations will be generated.

For example:
Your seven (7) numbers are 02 – 07 – 14 – 26 – 30 – 43 – 47. All the possible six-number combinations are:

02 – 07 – 14 – 26 – 30 – 43
02 – 07 – 14 – 26 – 30 – 47
02 – 07 – 14 – 26 – 43 – 47
02 – 07 – 14 – 30 – 43 – 47
02 – 07 – 26 – 30 – 43 – 47
02 – 14 – 26 – 30 – 43 – 47
07 – 14 – 26 – 30 – 43 – 47

The same principle applies to Systems 9, 10, 11 and 12. Refer to the table for the corresponding number of combinations per System Play and the cost. Remember, Php20.00 for every six-number combination.

System 5 Roll System 7 System 8 System 9 System 10 System 11 System 12
No. of Combinations 44 7 28 84 210 462 924
Cost (Peso) 880.00 140.00 560.00 1680.00 4,200.00 9,240.00 18,480.00
Lucky Pick

If you want to try any of the System Play(s) but you don’t have any number in mind, you can let the computer choose your numbers by playing Lucky Pick (LP).

Just mark your choice of System Play and Lucky Pick (LP) on your play slip.

For example:
5Roll, Lucky Pick – Mark 5R and LP on your play slip, pay Php880.00 for thirty-seven (37) combinations.
System 7, Lucky Pick – Mark SYS 7 and LP on your play slip, pay Php140.00 for seven (7) combinations.
The same principle applies to System 8, 9, 10, 11 and 12, Lucky Pick.
